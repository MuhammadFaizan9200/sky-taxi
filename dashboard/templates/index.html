Skybluetaxi

Book a taxi anywhere in the Netherlands

With skybluetaxi you travel to and from any location in the Netherlands and airports in Germany and Belgium. Every day the booking platform matches many travelers to one of the 5000 affiliate taxi drivers. These local taxi companies have been screened on experience, quality of service and required certificates. As a platform, Skybluetaxi has the TX-Keurmerk certificate: the national quality mark in the taxi industry.
Through skybluetaxi online booking platform you can easily book a taxi to airports, healthcare facilities, business appointments, festivals, hotels or day trips. You can choose from the following transport options: standard, luxury transport, electric transport, wheelchair taxi or a minibus for up to eight people. You are always transported in a private taxi by a certified taxi company. There are no extra costs for your luggage, and you can change your ride online or cancel it free of charge up to three hours before departure.
